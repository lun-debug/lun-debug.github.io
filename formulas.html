Quick Reference Table

This table provides the essential formulas needed for basic geometry calculations. Perfect for students and quick revision.

Shape Area Formula Perimeter / Circumference
Circle π × r² 2 × π × r
Square s² 4 × s
Triangle 0.5 × b × h a + b + c
Rectangle L × W 2 × (L + W)

Important Constants

Pi (π): Approximately 3.14159

In most basic geometry problems, using 3.14 is sufficient for calculations.

How to use these formulas:

  1. Identify the shape: Look at the properties (number of sides, angles).
  2. Determine known values: Find the length of the base, height, or radius.
  3. Check the units: Ensure all measurements are in the same units (e.g., all cm or all m).
  4. Calculate: Plug the numbers into the formulas above.
